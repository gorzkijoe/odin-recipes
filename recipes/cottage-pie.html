Fluffy Cottage pie!

Cottage pie is a delicious casserole recipe with a ground beef and vegetable filling that’s topped with a mountain of fluffy mashed potatoes.

This is what you will need ↓

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • 120ml milk
  • 50g butter
  • salt & ground pepper
  • olive oil
  • large onion
  • garlic cloves
  • 2 large carrots, diced
  • 2 celery, diced
  • 700g ground beef
  • 250ml beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons chopped rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
  • 150g frozen peas

Follow the recipe ↓

  1. Cook the potatoes.
  2. Mash the potatoes.
  3. Saute the onion and garlic.
  4. Add more veggies and meat.
  5. Add the flavor.
  6. Add the peas.
  7. Top with mashed potatoes.
  8. Bake.

You are done! Bon appetit!
